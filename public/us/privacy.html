1. Overview

Lexoria provides an AI-powered intake organization service for legal professionals. We handle personal data with care and only for legitimate service-related purposes.

2. Service description

The service structures consultation notes into organized sections (for example: facts, claims, issues, and follow-up checks). AI output is for workflow support and is not legal advice.

3. Information we collect

  • Account details such as email address and login credentials.
  • Usage records such as feature access and plan status.
  • Anonymous analytics events for product quality improvement.
  • Payment-related records through Stripe (we do not store full card numbers).

4. Consultation note handling

Do not input highly sensitive client-identifying details unless your internal policy permits doing so.
  • Input text is sent only for requested AI processing.
  • We do not sell consultation text or use it for advertising.
  • Data handling follows provider policies applicable to API processing.

5. How we use information

  • Service operation, account management, and support.
  • Reliability monitoring, abuse prevention, and troubleshooting.
  • Product improvement based on anonymized or aggregated trends.
  • Billing and subscription lifecycle management.

6. Third-party services

  • AI provider for processing intake text.
  • Stripe for payment processing.
  • Analytics providers (including Google Analytics) for usage measurement.

7. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies and related technologies for session continuity and anonymous analytics. You can control cookies in browser settings, though some features may be affected.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical safeguards, including encrypted transport where applicable. No internet system can guarantee absolute security.

9. Children

The service is intended for professional users and is not directed to children under 18.

10. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of account-related personal information where applicable law provides such rights.

11. Policy updates

We may revise this policy when legal requirements or service operations change. Material updates will be communicated appropriately.

12. Contact

support@lexoriaai.com
