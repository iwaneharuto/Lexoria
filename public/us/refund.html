Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Lexoria offers a 7-day free trial for new accounts. This page explains when billing begins, when refunds may be considered, and how to contact support.

1. 7-day free trial and billing start

  • Your trial begins when you activate a paid plan trial.
  • Billing begins automatically after the 7-day trial unless canceled before the trial ends.
  • You can cancel anytime during the trial from your account settings.

2. Refund policy principle

Because Lexoria is a SaaS service with immediate access to digital features, charges are generally non-refundable once a paid cycle has started.

We still review exceptional situations in good faith, especially where billing or service delivery was materially affected by platform-side issues.

3. Situations we may review for adjustment

  • Duplicate billing for the same account and billing period.
  • A clear technical fault on our side that prevented reasonable use after payment.
  • An obvious billing error caused by processing or plan transition mistakes.
Review does not guarantee a refund. Outcome depends on account and billing records.

4. Contact and handling timeline

Please contact support with your account email, invoice date, and a short description of the issue.

support@lexoriaai.com

Typical first response: within 1-2 business days.
