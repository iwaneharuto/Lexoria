Anti-Social Forces Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Lexoria has no relationship with anti-social forces, organized crime groups, or equivalent entities, and does not permit such involvement in any form.

1. Basic position

We maintain a strict non-association policy with anti-social forces and reject any direct or indirect involvement in management, transactions, or operations.

2. Representation and warranty

  • Users represent and warrant that neither they nor related parties are anti-social forces.
  • Users also represent that they will not engage in intimidation, fraud, or improper influence in relation to our service.

3. Refusal and termination

  • If anti-social involvement is suspected or confirmed, we may refuse service provision.
  • We may suspend or terminate accounts immediately without prior notice where necessary.
  • Where legally appropriate, we may cooperate with relevant authorities.
